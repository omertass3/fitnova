Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2025

Introduction

FitNova ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use FitNova.

Information We Collect

FitNova is designed with privacy in mind. We collect and process the following information:

  • Personal fitness data: Body type, age, gender, height, weight, body fat percentage, activity level, and fitness goals. This data is entered by you during onboarding.
  • Workout data: Exercise completion, XP earned, streak counts, and weekly progress.
  • Weight log entries: Weight entries you voluntarily log to track your progress.
  • Preferences: Language selection and app settings.

How We Store Your Data

All data is stored locally on your device only. FitNova does not transmit, upload, or share your personal data with any server, third party, or cloud service. Your data remains entirely on your device using local storage (AsyncStorage).

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, or photos.
  • We do not use cookies or web tracking technologies.

Third-Party Services

FitNova does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection to function.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You can delete all your data at any time using the "Reset All Data" option in the Profile tab.
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all associated data.
  • We have no ability to access, recover, or restore your data as it never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

FitNova does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The app is intended for general fitness use and does not target children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
